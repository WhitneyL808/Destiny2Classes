Warlock

Warlocks have long studied the Traveler, mastering some of its arcane energies. Its true purpose still remains a great mystery, but discovering truth has always driven you into the unknown. Now, our enemies stand between you and your efforts to recover our lost powers. Take up your weapon. Become legend.

  • Void: The Void Warlock's goal is to weaken enemies with grenades and survive with devour. The subclass also has access to healing and void debuffs.
  • Solar: Focused on setting the world on fire. The subclass has access to healing and damage buffs.
  • Arc: One of the game's most aggressive subclasses. It's focused on speed and damage, and allows players to rush through enemies and use every ability constantly.
  • Stasis: Darkness subclass that specializes in crowd control. Stasis Warlocks have a variety of ice-themed powers
  • Strand: Broodweavers are telekinetic sorcerers that manipulate the Weave, a psychic energy matrix that connects all things and minds in the universe.
